Shadow Shift Checklist

This form documents the shadow shift which is required of any person taking shifts for the Minerva detector. This form serves two purposes:
1. It provides documentation for experts to ensure that all shifters are adequately trained to take shift, and
2. It provides the shadowing shifter with an example of properly executed shift procedures that they may reference in advance of and during their future shifts.

This form should be filled out by the shadow shifter. If the shadow shifter doesn't have an ECL account, they shouldn't be taking a shadow shift.

Notes:
-This checklist should remain open for the duration of the shadow shift and be consulted/edited throghout the shift.
-It is the supervising shifter's responsibility to explain shift procedures accurately and thoroughly.
